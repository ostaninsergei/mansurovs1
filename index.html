Rust (English — "rust") is a computer game in the genre of survival simulator, was created by the independent British company Facepunch Studios, headed since 2014 by Harry Newman. The game was released in early access in December 2013, and on February 8, 2018, it was fully released for Windows and macOS. The PlayStation 4 and Xbox One versions, developed in collaboration with Double Eleven Studio, were released on May 21, 2021. Rust was originally created as a clone of DayZ with crafting elements similar to those in Minecraft.
